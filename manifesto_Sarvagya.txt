===== My Open Source Manifesto =====
Date: 29 March 2026
Author: Sarvagya

Every day, I rely on Python — a tool built not for profit,
but for the freedom to use, study, share and improve.
To me, freedom means Choice — and open source makes that real.
One day I will build A study planner and release it freely,
so that others can stand on my shoulders, just as I have stood
on the shoulders of those who came before me.
Open source is not just a license. It is a philosophy.
